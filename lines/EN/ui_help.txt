Planet consists of provinces, they can be owned by players. Yours are player{blue} ones, the zanarthians{blue} and jadians{red} are of your opponents.

player{[RMB]} -- select your province

player{[Shift + RMB]} -- multiple selection

player{[G]} -- hire army for gold

player{[A + RMB]}/player{[LMB]} -- attack or move

player{[P]} -- pause
